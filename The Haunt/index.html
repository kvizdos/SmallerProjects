About Us

Halloween, or All Hallows' Eve, occurs every year on October 31st and originates from the ancient Celtic festival of Samhain, when people would wear costumes and light fires to keep the ghosts away. Now, it's all about the horror of a single night.

You may want to think again before registering.. and if you do decide to continue, make sure you have your waivers signed...

The doors open at 9:00 PM every day. Come prepared. Every night is different.
